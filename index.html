Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

The years between the ages of 3 and 6 are a time of rapid development for children, known as early childhood development (ECD). During this time, children develop their physical, social, emotional, cognitive, and language skills.

Elementary School

An elementary school is a school that provides the first part of a child's education. It is also known as a primary school or grade school.

Middle (10-16) Years

The years between 10 and 19 are generally considered the period of adolescence, which is a time of rapid physical, cognitive, and psychosocial growth. This is a unique stage of human development that's important for laying the foundation for good health.
